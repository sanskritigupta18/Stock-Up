Feature of Stock Up

Automatic Backup

All the data stored in the GST invoicing software is automatically backed up whenever the phone is connected to the internet.

Offline Billing

The billing software not only works in online mode but is also available in offline mode with limited features.

Easy Data Transfer

For users migrating from other billing softwares, the stock up application provides an easy migration option.

Item Library

Stock up software with GST has a standard item library of 1 lakh+ FMCG & Pharma items.

Send Business Card

The Stock up application provides users with an option to create professional business cards & share them on WhatsApp with their customers/vendors.

Whatsapp Support

The billing app has a separate ‘Help & Support’ function to help users with FAQs and video tutorials on ‘How to’ use the app features.
